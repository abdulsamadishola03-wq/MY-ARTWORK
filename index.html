Artist Statement

My work explores the human face as a landscape of emotion, memory, and identity. Through the humble tools of pencil and ballpoint pen, I seek to capture not just likeness, but the intangible essence that makes each subject uniquely themselves.

I am drawn to portraiture because it demands both technical precision and emotional sensitivity. Every line, every shadow, every subtle gradation of tone is a conversation between artist and subject—a dialogue that transcends the physical to touch something deeper.

Working primarily in monochrome allows me to focus on what truly matters: the architecture of the face, the play of light and shadow, the quiet revelations found in a glance or smile. Each portrait is an act of observation, patience, and ultimately, reverence for the human form.

— Samk

About the Artist

Samk specializes in realistic portraits using pencil and ballpoint pen, capturing the essence and emotion of subjects through meticulous detail and masterful use of line and shadow. Each piece is a study in patience, precision, and the timeless art of drawing.
